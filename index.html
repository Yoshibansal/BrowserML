Training in progress

When you open console you can see the summary of model as well as training stats for every epoch. This model is a simplest model with just one layer having one neuron. Trained on a data X = [-1.0, 0.0, 1.0, 2.0, 3.0, 4.0] and Y = [-3.0, -1.0, 2.0, 3.0, 5.0, 7.0].

This model get trained on browser itself.
